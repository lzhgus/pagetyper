# PageTyper Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

PageTyper turns article pages into typing practice in your browser.

## Data We Process

PageTyper reads text from the current web page only to provide its typing practice modes. The text is processed locally in your browser and is not sent to a server.

PageTyper stores:

- your extension settings, such as default mode, reward effects, sound, and stats preferences;
- local typing milestone stats, streaks, and badges.

## Data Sharing

PageTyper does not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

PageTyper does not use analytics, advertising, tracking pixels, or remote code.

## Storage

Settings are stored with Chrome extension sync storage. Milestone stats and badges are stored with Chrome extension local storage.

## Limited Use

PageTyper uses page text and local extension data only to provide and improve its single purpose: turning readable article text into configurable typing practice.

The use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Chrome Web Store User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

## Contact

For questions, open an issue in the PageTyper GitHub repository.
